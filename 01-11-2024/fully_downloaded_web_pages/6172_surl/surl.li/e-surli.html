Why does e.surl.li exist?

Short link service surl.li has shortened millions of long links, thousands of them shortened by public, non-profit organizations, the social importance of whose work is difficult to overestimate. We decided to give the full functionality of the commercial platform to such organizations - for free!

Users who are not affiliated with .gov and .edu

If you are also involved with a non-profit, charitable organization, or work for an educational institution, please submit a request to use the free e.surl.li service. Are you a commercial organization, blogger or marketer? Thanks to you, we exist, it is for you that we develop the main service and implement changes. We will be glad to welcome you to the personal account of the surl.li user and provide the best conditions and functionality.
