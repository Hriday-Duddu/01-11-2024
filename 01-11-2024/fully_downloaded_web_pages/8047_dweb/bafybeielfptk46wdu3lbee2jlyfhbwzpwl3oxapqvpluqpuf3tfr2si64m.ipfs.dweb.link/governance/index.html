Aave Governance

Aave is a fully decentralized, community governed protocol by the AAVE token-holders. AAVE token-holders collectively discuss, propose, and vote on upgrades to the protocol. AAVE token-holders (Ethereum network only) can either vote themselves on new proposals or delagate to an address of choice. To learn more check out the Governance documentation.
