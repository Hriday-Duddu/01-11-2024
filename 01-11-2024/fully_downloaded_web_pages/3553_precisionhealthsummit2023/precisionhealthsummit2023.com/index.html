Precision Health Summit

December 11-13, 2023 | Paris, France

On behalf of Microsoft, we are delighted to invite you to the Precision Health Summit in Paris, France, taking place December 12-13, 2023. Focusing on empowering a new era of precision health discovery and enabling the last mile of healthcare delivery, the summit will host 200 leading Healthcare innovators across Healthcare Providers, Payors and Life sciences.

We look forward to bringing together clinicians, researchers, national and regional authorities, technologists and digital leaders to highlight synergies and the convergence across Healthcare providers, Academic Medical Centers, Research Organizations, Pharma companies and Health Payors. Together, we will share progress and insights across the customer and partner perspective to outline innovation, foster peer-to-peer inspiration and cultivate learning.
